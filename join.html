Join the ring.

Open to anyone who has competed in competitive debate — any format, any level, anywhere in the world.

Who can join?

If you've competed in competitive debate — British Parliamentary, World Schools, policy, Lincoln-Douglas, public forum, or any other format, at any level — you're eligible. High school, university, open circuit, all fine.

Your content can be a blog, newsletter, YouTube channel, podcast, or any other platform where you publish original long-form work. Social media profiles and short-form content aren't a fit.

After you submit

We'll review your submission and get back to you by email. If approved, your site is added to the ring automatically and your webring links start working.

Adding webring links to your site

Once you're in, it's encouraged (but not required) to add navigation links so visitors can hop through the ring. They're just static links — no script, no embed — so they work on any platform including WordPress and Squarespace.

Once approved, you'll see a "your webring links" section on your entry in the ring with a one-click copy button. Paste those links anywhere — your site footer, Substack about page, YouTube description, wherever.
